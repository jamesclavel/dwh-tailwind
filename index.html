How Davao.Web.Hosting Started...

The year is 2014, when "co-working space" is still new in the city. I joined a group of individuals to start one. The group has different skills set and my part is to setup the space for internet connection sharing and manage the group's website.

At the same time, I started Davao.Web.Hosting as a pet project and the group's concept was the first client. Although the idea was good, the "co-working space" didn't live long. On the other hand, DWH has gained more supporters and I focused more in providing quick resolution for support requests and prompt response when clients have questions. A pet-project run by a web-developer for web-developers and the likes.

We started with Reseller Hosting back in 2014, fast-forward to 2022 we will be providing web-hosting solution using VPS (virtual-private-server) using OpenSource Technologies.

Our Services

Domain Registration

Getting the right domain name for your website is crucial when it comes to memory recall. It must be simple, easy to remember and catchy at the same time.Once you have the perfect domain name, we can register it in your behalf.

Web Hosting

You can focus on your content, we'll handle the heavy lifting of setting up your VPS. In the past, we used shared-hosting solution - but why share your resources when you can have scalability power using Virtual Private Servers.

Website Migration

Sometimes, we're not happy anymore with our current web hosting provider and wanted to move on. If you're moving in to DWH, we can definitely help you with that! From domain transfer to web and database migration - we are here to provide assistance.

The same goes if you're moving away from us - no hard feelings.

Email Migration

Does your website have multiple email addresses setup to it and you're planning to move-in with DWH? Worry no more, we got you covered! We'll assess the scope of the migration process and maybe we can do it for FREE.
